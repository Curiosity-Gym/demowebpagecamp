The following is the schedule and description of the sessions during the camp.

Commanding Light

Light plays a vital role in our daily lives. Daily activities, from scanning items at a grocery store, clicking a selfie, to watching TV and reading books involves light.
This course is aimed at understanding what light is, how it behaves,how can we manipulate light to expand our sense of vision so that we can see the farthest of objects to the smallest of things.
Participants will be provided parts in kit form to assemble their own telescope and microscope.

Digital StoryTelling

Creating stories is one of our favorite and most important activities. It gives meaning to our other activities, integrating them into a whole. There are patterns in stories and almost all knowledge that we create, takes the form of stories. Some we believe in more than others.
During this workshop, we will engage in games, creative writing, critical thinking, conversations to uncover feelings and experiences of personal importance. Then using Scratch Software we will create digital stories that can be shared with the world.
Throughout the session we will discuss concepts from various storytelling traditions like arts, science, philosophy, technology and more.
Children will subtly engage in logic, algorithms and computer programming.

Build Apps for Android - the fun way

With the ubiquity of smartphones and tablets, it has become commonplace to add apps to product offerings. This course exposes the participants to the non-programming and visual way of creating applications for Android Platform using AppInventor
Participants will use AppInventor to learn how to create functional and beautiful apps that can help to create value in their projects.
Participants need to come with their own laptops and Android Smartphones, with a modern browser.

Build and Fly a pocket drone

This course is aimed at young robotics enthusiasts who want to take their hobby and skill to the next level.
Participants will learn how a drone works, what are its parts, be able to construct the drone, and then successfully fly it.
Participants will be provided with a kit to build, and guided on how to assemble the drone, which can be purchased for an additional cost.
Participants need to carry an Android phone with them, which will be used to install a certified application to control the drone.
